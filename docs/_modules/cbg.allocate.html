allocate(patients: List[Dict[str, Any]]) → None[source]¶

Allocates the patients by using the distance to a location as a metric. The patients are assigned to the closest available location until the location is full for the week, moving to the next closest location. This strategy is not good for machine utilisation. :param patients: The list of patients to schedule.
